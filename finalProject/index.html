Why You Should Adopt A Pet?

I chose this topic because so many animals are getting abused every day. Most people go out to buy pets in pet shops and don't think about adopting animals from the animal shelter. Either they don't know about the shelters in their area or they simply don't want to go through the trouble. What they don't realize is that when you adopt an animal, they will be saving money and save a pet's life.

"Sad cat happy mom" by Mike. Licensed under CC by 2.0

Here's a list of the top five reasons why you should adopt

  1. You will end up saving a life.
  2. You'll get a new loving family member.
  3. Rescue pets are intelligent, emotional beings who deserve a chance at a long, happy life.
  4. Many rescue pets are trained and have recieved veterinary care.
  5. You will save money.
